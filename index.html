Definitions of dibble

  1. noun a wooden hand tool with a pointed end; used to make holes in the ground for planting seeds or bulbs

    synonyms: dibber

    see more

  2. verb plant with a wooden hand tool

    “dibble Spring bulbs”

    see more

  3. verb make a hole with a wooden hand tool

    “dibble the ground”

    see more
